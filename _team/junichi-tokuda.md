Junichi Tokuda is a research scientist at the Department of Radiology, Brigham and Womenâ€™s Hospital (BWH), Boston, MA. He is specialized in the development and clinical translation of novel software and robotics technologies for image-guided interventions. At BWH, he works with his clinical collaborators primarily on MRI-guided interventions of pelvic and abdominal organs. As a Principal Investigator, he is leading several research projects which are funded by the National Institutes of Health and industry. Before he joined the BWH/HMS community in 2007, he received undergraduate and graduate training at the University of Tokyo, Japan, and obtained a B.S. in Engineering and M.S. and Ph.D. in Information and Science and Technology. He is currently an Associate Professor of Radiology at Harvard Medical School.
